ABOUT US

DP Design is an Ireland-based technical textile company specializing in the supply of high-performance healthcare and hospitality textile solutions for institutional markets.

The company focuses on technically engineered textiles designed to meet the demanding operational requirements of hospitals, healthcare institutions, industrial laundries, nursing homes, hospitality groups, and institutional buyers across Ireland, Europe, and international markets.

With strategically established manufacturing partnerships and production capabilities in Asia, DP Design combines competitive global manufacturing strength with European quality expectations, compliance standards, and sustainable sourcing practices.

DP Design is positioned to support the growing European demand for high-quality reusable healthcare textiles driven by:

* Increasing healthcare infrastructure expansion

* Rising infection control standards

* Sustainability-focused procurement policies

* Industrial laundry optimization

* EU environmental compliance requirements

* Growing preference for durable reusable textile systems

OUR VISION

To become a trusted European supplier of innovative, sustainable, and technically advanced healthcare and hospitality textile solutions.

OUR MISSION

DP Design is committed to delivering high-performance technical textile solutions that combine quality, compliance, sustainability, durability, and operational efficiency.

Our mission is to support healthcare and hospitality industries through technically engineered textile products that enhance hygiene, safety, patient comfort, environmental responsibility, and long-term value.
